Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026

Princess Housekeeping ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking or contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, and property address. We do not collect payment card details — payments are handled in person or via trusted third-party payment apps.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your cleaning booking
  • To contact you via phone or WhatsApp
  • To send booking reminders
  • To improve our services

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our service. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. Contact us at princesshousekeeping@yahoo.com or 067 920 3787.

Your safety matters

Security is our priority

We are a proudly women-led business. Every measure below protects both our clients and our team.

🪪

ID Verified Team

Every team member carries a verified ID badge at all times. You can request to see ID before any clean begins. No ID shown — no entry.

📍

Live Check-In System

Our team checks in via WhatsApp to a trusted contact on every job arrival and departure. If no check-out is received, a welfare check is triggered immediately.

📸

Before & After Photos

We photograph the space on arrival and on completion. This protects you from false damage claims and protects our team from false accusations.

📋

Booking Records

Every booking is logged with your name, address and contact number. Records are kept securely. No anonymous jobs accepted — ever.

🚫

Right to Decline

Our team reserves the right to decline or leave any job where they feel unsafe. Clients who are aggressive or inappropriate are blacklisted immediately.

🆘

Emergency Contact

A trusted emergency contact holds our team's full schedule at all times. In any incident, they can provide job location and client details to authorities.
