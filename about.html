👑 Our Mission

Our housekeeping company is proudly 100% Black-owned, with a deep and unwavering commitment to women empowerment. We specialise in deep cleaning, spring cleaning, and after-event cleanups — bringing thorough, professional care to homes and businesses across Cape Town and Durban. But every clean is more than just a job to us. It is an opportunity to create employment for women and help them grow within the cleaning industry. Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional service, every single time, while uplifting and championing the incredible women who make it all possible.

The Princess
Founder & CEO

Welcome to Princess Housekeeping. I started this business with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to come home — or walk into their space — and feel at peace. A truly clean space is not a luxury. It is something every person deserves.

Growing up, I learned that the way you care for a space reflects the way you care for people. That belief is at the heart of everything we do. Every clean we deliver is done with intention, with attention to detail, and with genuine pride in our work.

From our very first client to our three-hundred-and-fiftieth, the standard has never changed. We show up on time. We do the job properly. We leave your space better than we found it. And we do it all with a smile.

Whether you need your home freshened up, your BnB turned around between guests, your office spotless, or your newly built property cleared of dust — we are here for you. Cape Town and Durban, every day, 8 to 5.

Thank you for trusting Princess Housekeeping. We do not take that trust lightly. We earn it — one sparkling space at a time. 👑

With love, The Princess

How we got here

Our journey

✨
The beginning

A dream and a cleaning cloth

Princess Housekeeping was founded with nothing more than a passion for clean spaces, a strong work ethic, and the belief that South Africans deserve better home care services.

First 100 homes cleaned

Word spread fast. Clients referred us to their friends, family and colleagues. We expanded to BnB turnovers, offices, and after-construction cleans — all from genuine referrals.
